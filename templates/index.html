Download Your QR-Code

QR Code is a two-dimensional version of the barcode, typically made up of black and white pixel patterns. Now, it has found its way into mobile marketing with the widespread adoption of smartphones. "QR" stands for "Quick Response", which refers to the instant access to the information hidden in the Code.

Creating QR-Code from Link

This means that even if the QR code sustains minor damage or distortion, it can still be accurately scanned and decoded, effectively turning the link to the QR code

Why do I need Vcard generator?

Having a vCard QR code on your business card or other marketing materials can make it easy for people to quickly and easily access your contact information. By simply scanning the QR code, potential customers or clients can save your contact details directly into their phone's address book. This can save time and reduce errors that can occur when manually entering contact information. Additionally, a vCard QR code can give your business a more modern and tech-savvy images, making it stand out from competitors who are still relying on traditional printed materials.

How do i scan QR Codes?

Depending on your device, you might already have a built-in QR Code reader or scanner. Open the camera app on your mobile phone and hold it over a Code for a few seconds until a notification pops up. If this doesn’t happen, check your settings and see if QR Code scanning is enabled. Still not working? Don’t worry, all you have to do now is install third-party QR Code readers from your app stores.
